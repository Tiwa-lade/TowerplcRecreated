CORPORATE INFORMATION

Tower Aluminium Group is part of the Africa wide present - Clovis Group.

The Tower Aluminium Group commenced with Aluminium Kitchenware in 1959 with its flagship company Tower Aluminium (Nig.) Plc in Lagos, Nigeria & over the years expanded its operations, investments & commitments in West Africa. The Group's product range encompasses Patterned & Stucco Coils, Color Coated Coils, Roofing Sheets, Cookware & Extruded Profiles.
Tower Aluminium Group, the single largest vertically integrated Aluminium down stream producer is a pioneer & a leader in West Africa. Tower is a part of the Clovis Group ,with presence in Nigeria, Benin, , Ghana, Ivory Coast, Burkina Faso, Mali & Guinee Conakry . Tower prides for excellence in customer care & serving the society at large since 1959.
